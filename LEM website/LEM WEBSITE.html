Driver License Progression

The Four Tiers of Certification

The certification system governs eligibility for all championships, enforcing safety and providing a clear, transparent progression path based on skill, consistency, and racecraft.

BRONZE

Entry-Level

  • Basic Racecraft & Safe Driving
  • Consistent Lap Times
  • Club Spec Cup & National Challenges

SILVER

Intermediate

  • Clean Overtakes & Defensive Driving
  • Tyre/Fuel Management
  • National Touring Cars & GT4 Challenge

GOLD

Advanced Endurance

  • Multi-Class Awareness
  • Pit Strategy & Adaptability
  • WEC, ELMS, IMSA (GT3/Hypercar)

PLATINUM

Professional

  • Mastery of All Disciplines
  • Advanced Team Leadership & Strategy
  • F1 World Championship & Hypercar World Cup

Strategy & Competitive Mindset

Meticulous Car Setup

Success begins with the setup. Prioritize tire pressure and temperature for long-run consistency over qualifying speed, especially in endurance formats.

Practice Regimen

Teams must simulate full race stints to understand the precise rate of tire wear, and practice seamless pit stop procedures during organized sessions.

Strategic Planning

Develop a pre-planned strategy for BoP (Balance of Performance) and Success Ballast impacts. Tactical fuel management and pit windows are paramount.
